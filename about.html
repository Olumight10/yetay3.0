About Yetay Memorial Project

Raising a Word-Centered Generation

Our Vision

To steer up positive hunger and a burning desire for God's word amongst children and young adults across West Africa, assembling young minds under a neutral roof to represent Christ in fair and equitable Bible-based engagements.

Our Mission

To foster unity in the body of Christ and promote deeper engagement with God's Word through the Yetay Interdenominational Bible Quiz Competition, strengthening the good works of the church community amidst worldly distractions.

The Story So Far

The Yetay Memorial Project was birthed out of an undiluted passion for revival amongst the youth. Initiated by the Banner Unto Nations Foundation, it started as a small gathering of kingdom-minded believers who saw the need to ground the next generation in scripture.

Over the past editions, we have witnessed tremendous growth. Churches from various denominations have come together, setting aside doctrinal differences to celebrate the pure, unadulterated Word of God. Our focus changes per season based on the leading of the Holy Spirit, ensuring that every edition addresses the spiritual needs of the current generation.

As we approach Yetay 3.0, our team of young, diversified men and women remains committed to serving God through this platform, building a communal effort to grow the word bank in our children and teens.
