Introduction to Banner Unto Nation Foundation

We hope this letter finds you well. We are writing on behalf of Banner Unto Nations Foundation, a non-governmental organization committed to proffering sustainable solution to environmental, social and economic challenges in West Africa. We achieve this by maintaining different key projects initiatives such as the Pan African Women Submit (PAWS), Just One Child Education Initiatives, Orphan and Vulnerable Children Outreaches etc. We are glad to introduce you to our most recent initiative the Yetay Memorial Project, this is a faith-based project created to steer up positive hunger and burning desire for God's word amongst children and young adults.

This initiative strives to make a positive impact in Port Harcourt and immediate environment. To this end, we are keen to establish connections and collaborations with word-based ministry like yours. Our major flagship program is the Yetay Interdenominational Bible Quiz Competition across age groups. This competition is poised to assemble young minds that has been consistently exposed to God's word under a neutral roof to represent Christ and their denomination in a fair and equitable Bible based quiz competition. The focus of the Bible quiz is released based on the dealing and leading of the Holy Spirit per season and per session.

We firmly believe that this collaboration is key to achieving meaningful and lasting change in this city. This partnership would further strengthen the good works of the church community, foster love, unity and raise a word centred generation in the midst of many worldly distractions. Our team is a diversified pool of young men and women that have undiluted passion for revival amongst the youth and also serving God through varying denominational platforms. We are particularly interested in your buy-in of this vision, ensuring your church is ably represented for our rounds of competitions, and any other general support as necessary. We believe our combined efforts would yield effectual and tremendous results.

Please feel free to reach out to us at 08130585886, 08063752650 and 07062513337 to enquire or to schedule a physical meeting for further engagement. Thank you for your kind acceptance, and we look forward to a fruitful relationship and communal effort in growing the word bank in the next generation. We are excited about this move of God, and we count on your support for the delivery of this mandate.

We have attached the guidelines of the Competition and a Letter of Invitation.
